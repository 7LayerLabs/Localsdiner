Welcome to Locals Diner

For years, we've been serving the Dracut community with the values our father taught us: give them great food, great portions at a great price. Every meal we serve honors that family legacy.

We're proud to keep this tradition alive, welcoming locals and visitors alike to experience homemade comfort food in a warm, friendly atmosphere.

Community-Centered

At Locals Diner, you're not just a customer, you're family. We believe in quality ingredients, generous servings, and affordable prices that bring our community together.

From hearty breakfasts, traditional lunches and homemade pies and desserts, everything is made with care and served with a smile.
